BC PNP Occupation List 2023 (Updated)

By Kawal Sharma22 November 2022No Comments
Share Tweet Share Pin

“How do I know if my TEER NOC code is included in the BC PNP occupation list?”

In order to answer this question, you must decide whether you are applying for BC PNP Skills Immigration, Express Entry BC, or BC PNP Tech pathways for skilled workers. The following guide contains the updated BC PNP occupation list 2022 for each British Columbia Provincial Nominee Program.

BC PNP Occupation Categories

Most of the skilled workers apply for the BC PNP Skills Immigration stream. The stream is further divided into occupation-based programs. Additionally, there are some priority occupations under the BC PNP. These BC PNP occupation categories are given below:

  • BC PNP Skilled Worker
  • BC PNP Health Authority
  • BC PNP Entry Level and Semi-Skilled
  • BC PNP International Graduate
  • BC PNP Tech
  • BC PNP Priority Occupations
  • Express Entry BC

Let’s discuss the eligible occupations for BC PNP Skills Immigration Stream.

BC PNP Health Authrority Occupation List

BC PNP Health Authority is a special category under British Columbia PNP for health care professionals. This stream is open to any occupation. Additionally, to apply under this stream you must have a full-time, indeterminate, and permanent job offer from one of the B.C. public health authorities. You can contact Health Match BC for free recruitment services.

Express Entry BC Occupation List

When the BC Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP) was introduced by IRCC in 2015, it was aligned with Express Entry. The Express Entry BC (EEBC) allows eligible applicants to receive priority in the processing of their applications for the BC PNP. The following streams are eligible for BC PNP Express Entry:

  • EEBC Skilled Workers
  • EEBC Health Authority
  • EEBC International Post Graduates
  • EEBC International Graduates

The eligible occupations for the BC PNP Express Entry categories are the same as the corresponding Skills Immigration categories. For example, occupations eligible for the BC PNP Skilled Worker category of skills immigration are also eligible for the EEBC Skilled Worker category.

You must also be eligible through one of the three federal immigration programs to apply through Express Entry BC. These programs are Federal Skilled Worker Program, Federal Skilled Trades Program, and Canadian Experience Class.
